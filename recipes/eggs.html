Scrambled Eggs

Description

A dish prepared by stirring, whipping, or beating eggs together then heating.

Dish is generally served with toast, bacon, sausage, and other breakfast foods.

Ingredients

  • Desired amount of eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Choice of hot sauce

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into bowl
  2. Whisk eggs until egg whites and yolks are thorougly mixed
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Add butter to pan and heat until butter is melted, so as to prevent eggs from sticking to pan
  5. Pour egg mixture into pan
  6. Cook on stovetop applying medium heat for three to four minutes until mixture has coagulated
  7. Discontinue application of heat and transfer eggs from pan to plate
  8. Apply desired amount of hot sauce or salsa
