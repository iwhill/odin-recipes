Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Dish with a tomato based sauce with minced meat served over spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Italian Sausage
  • Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Boil spaghetti in pot of water on stovetop
  2. Pour noodles into strainer once thoroughly cooked
  3. Cook italian sausage in pan while using cooking spoon to mince
  4. Add tomato sauce and garlic once sausage is thorougly cooked
  5. Serve noodles on plate and add tomato sauce with the sausage on top of noodles
  6. Add paremsan cheese if desired
